Seamlessly Integrating Software QA and Testing with Yottron Solutions

Requirement Analysis

During this phase, we analyze your requirements, goals, and constraints to define the scope and objectives of the QA and testing project.

Test Planning

This phase involves creating a comprehensive test plan, including strategies, resources, schedules, and risk assessment, based on the analysis conducted in the previous phase.

Test Design

In this phase, detailed test cases, test scripts, and test data are designed to cover all aspects of the software's functionality and performance.

Test Execution

During test execution, the prepared test cases and scripts are executed to identify any defects or issues, ensuring the software meets the required quality standards.

Defect Reporting

Once defects are identified, they are reported, tracked, and prioritized for resolution. Detailed defect reports help in understanding and addressing issues effectively.

Test Closure

After successful test execution and defect resolution, the test closure phase involves summarizing and analyzing the testing process, ensuring all objectives are met.
